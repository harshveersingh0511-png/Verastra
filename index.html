03 The Gap

Every other major asset has a model. Yours doesn't.

Equity has DCFs. Real estate has cap rates. Fixed income has yield curves. Your career — a multi-crore, multi-decade position with embedded tax, FX, volatility, and optionality — is the only one underwritten on cohort gossip.

Decision 01 Education capex

Two years out, ₹70L of tuition, and forgone compensation against a post-tax payback. At what discount rate does the MBA stop clearing?

Decision 02 Relocation arbitrage

₹85L in Bangalore, ₹85L in Singapore. Post-tax, post-rent, post-FX, post-COL — which is the larger real position?

Decision 03 Founder optionality

Stay at the firm on ₹1.2Cr, or take half the cash and 0.5% equity. At what exit value does the option finally price in?

Decision 04 Path portfolio

Deepen one discipline, or stack a second. Which compounds the higher premium over fifteen years — and at what volatility cost?
